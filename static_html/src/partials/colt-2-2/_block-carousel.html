LIVE COLT'S SUCCESS BEHAVIOURS

Test drive each of these to make them a part of who you are as a Coltie.

GET TO KNOW YOUR CUSTOMERS

Even if you are not in a customer-facing role, you will be supporting someone who is. Your colleagues in different countries and functions are also your ‘customers’. Strengthen these relationships, listen, observe and collaborate.

PRIORITISE TASKS AND GET IT TOGETHER

Doing this allows us to smash our goals.

SPEND QUALITY TIME WITH YOUR LINE MANAGER

Get to know each other so that realistic expectations can be set to help you be successful.

SET YOURSELF QUICK WINS

You have a lot on your plate in your first month but by knowing your manager’s priorities, you can set short-term objectives and show you support them.

KEEP AN OPEN MIND

Despite your experience and skillset, you still have a lot to learn about your new role and environment. Remember, you can’t do it alone so ask for help and thank people when they do help you.
