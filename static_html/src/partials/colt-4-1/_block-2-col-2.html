CREATE AND RELATE

Healthy workplace relationships are very important at Colt. They are the foundation of the interpersonal skills and behaviours Colties need to understand each other through 'speaking the same language' — and, no, we don’t just mean English!

THE BUILDING BLOCKS

The building blocks for healthy work relationships are:

Get to know your colleague’s role.

Understand how your roles interlink.

Share information and ideas across the organisation.

THE KEY

The key lies in being able to communicate and interact effectively with your colleagues. When you are able not only to hear them clearly but also to interpret what they’re saying, you will be able to respond positively. An essential ingredient for doing this is being able to recognise your colleagues’ personality types.
