GOOD COMMUNICATION TIPS FROM THE MONROE MODEL

Use these five steps as a technique to organise your communication in a way that inspires people into action and allows for maximum impact from your message.

1. Get Attention
Grab the attention of your audience. Use storytelling, humour, shock statistics or whatever it takes, but make sure they sit up and take notice.

2. Establish the Need
Show your audience that there is a problem and that what is currently happening is no longer good enough. They need to make a change and take action.

3. Satisfy the Need
Introduce your solution to your audience. Be sure to give specifics and workable solutions to your audience’s problem.

4. Visualise the Future
This is where you describe to your audience what will happen if they follow your solution, or if they don’t and choose to do nothing. The closer you stick to reality, the easier it will be to motivate your audience to take action.

5. Action
Lastly, make sure the audience has specific details of the actions they need to take to solve the problem.
