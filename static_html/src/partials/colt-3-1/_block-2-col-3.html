GETTING TO YES

How do you get to a YES outcome with your customers?

Start by being curious. First find out everything that is available and can be offered to meet what they want.

Price may not be the only negotiating tool you have. You may be able to offer a different service level or a longer-term contract.

Either of these could be as valuable as price to both Colt and your customer.

So, be sure to prepare well so that you know everything your customer would be interested in before you even start negotiating.

Successful negotiators possess an equal amount of competitive and collaborative skills. Develop these skills to become more successful.
