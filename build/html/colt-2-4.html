ENGAGE TO SUCCEED

It’s easy to know whether an employee is engaged or not.

If they’re engaged, their loyalty, commitment and motivation will be strong and visible in everything they do.

Engagement is the EMOTIONAL CONNECTION you have with your colleagues and your company.

Colties are happiest when their needs are being met.

It’s much easier for you as a Coltie to be productive and engaged when you know your work is valued and appreciated. Your natural hunger for recognition gets a super-charge of motivation when your loyalty and hard work are acknowledged, and you see your efforts contributing to Colt’s goals.

Your dedication and motivation to succeed consistently reconnect you to Colt.

THE RIGHT ENVIRONMENT

With the right environment and tools for your job, you will be able to do your best work.

Business systems and processes that also add value to your role and that support your well-being will actively strengthen your engagement.

GOALS AND STRATEGIES

Make it a priority to know Colt’s strategic objectives and goals. Encourage your colleagues to contribute.

When you know how your own work is contributing to and building Colt’s success, your engagement levels will strengthen.

HOW COLT BUILDS ENGAGEMENT

Fun, social activities like quizzes and sports days

Corporate Social Responsibility programmes in every country where we have offices

A Resilience Campaign that encourages and supports you at work and at home

HOW TO BE ENGAGED IN WHAT YOU DO, EVERY DAY

Be proactive.

Know your role and the goals expected of you.

Use your voice and speak up – give feedback through the available channels and ambassadors in your country.
