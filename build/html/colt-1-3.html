READY, SET, GO!

After being at Colt for a few days, you’ll start to feel more comfortable. Your onboarding journey will continue for a few months. How will you make the most of this experience? You could just go according to the onboarding structure, or…you could take ownership of your experience.

What’s the difference? In both cases you’ll be following process. But, if you take things into your own hands, you’ll also find yourself settling into the Colt culture a lot faster.

Have a look at these 7 tips to help you fast track your onboarding journey:

TIP 1
PAY ATTENTION TO HOW WE TREAT CUSTOMERS

They are a priority at Colt, and the faster you learn how to engage with them, the sooner you’ll be able to do this yourself.

Remember: Customers are not just external. Colleagues and managers are internal customers.

TIP 2
GET A FEEL FOR THE TEAM

The way you relate to your colleagues will impact how well you work. Make an effort to engage with them, even in small ways (like grabbing them a coffee).

TIP 3
THINK 'CHANGE'

You’re still new, but that shouldn’t stop you from making notes on how you could use your fresh perspective and innovative touch. As soon as you’re ready, you’ll be able to start making a positive impact.

TIP 4
Take note of how every action creates value at Colt.

You’ve come across Colt’s success framework and now you need to get familiar it’s behaviours. As you get up and running, align the things you do with our bigger success picture.

TIP 5
SET AN EXAMPLE

Even if you’re not in a formal leadership role, you can lead those around you. During your first weeks, this may seem difficult, but it could be as simple as acting in a way that is proactive, honest and enthusiastic.

TIP 6
IDEAS ARE IMPORTANT

If you have a brainwave, mention it to peers or a manager. They will be able to tell you if there is a way it can be put into practice in the Colt context.

TIP 7
GOAL-SETTING STARTS EARLY

As you learn about Colt, you should plan your personal goals and figure out how they align with the company’s bigger goal. This will keep you on-track to achieve success.

YOU CAN DO IT!

It may be early days for you at Colt, but remember, wherever you are in your journey, it’s always the right time to be proactive, to take initiative and to create real value for yourself.
