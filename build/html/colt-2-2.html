YOUR COLTIE JOURNEY, BEYOND THE FIRST WEEK...

So, you’ve settled in at Colt and the basics have become familiar. As a new Coltie, you’re able to start focusing on getting up to speed with your role and responsibilities - you’re even able to put your experience, knowledge and skills to good use.

YOU'VE GOT THIS!

As you get more confident, be sure to do the following:

LIVE COLT'S SUCCESS BEHAVIOURS

Test drive each of these to make them a part of who you are as a Coltie.

GET TO KNOW YOUR CUSTOMERS

Even if you are not in a customer-facing role, you will be supporting someone who is. Your colleagues in different countries and functions are also your ‘customers’. Strengthen these relationships, listen, observe and collaborate.

PRIORITISE TASKS AND GET IT TOGETHER

Doing this allows us to smash our goals.

SPEND QUALITY TIME WITH YOUR LINE MANAGER

Get to know each other so that realistic expectations can be set to help you be successful.

SET YOURSELF QUICK WINS

You have a lot on your plate in your first month but by knowing your manager’s priorities, you can set short-term objectives and show you support them.

KEEP AN OPEN MIND

Despite your experience and skillset, you still have a lot to learn about your new role and environment. Remember, you can’t do it alone so ask for help and thank people when they do help you.

THE FIRST 90 DAYS

By now, you’re feeling like family. You know what’s expected of you, you’re making regular cups of coffee, and you’ve started delivering quality work. At this stage, it’s all about you owning your role and your work.

KEEP ON KEEPING ON...

You know the team dynamics and you’re hopefully building new relationships and strong bonds with your colleagues.

It’s now all up to you to establish yourself in the team and the company as a true Coltie. Show what you’re capable of and own your success.

Keep your focus on:

SETTING GOALS AND OBJECTIVES TO SEE WHAT YOU’VE GOT

You feel more comfortable which means it’s time to start reaching higher and further. Be guided by Colt’s winning attitude of smashing goals as you up your pace. Incredible professional and personal growth will be sure to follow!

ASSESSING AND ADJUSTING YOUR BOUNDARIES

You were probably flexible with your boundaries during your first few months. Now it’s time to reset them to give you more energy to do your best work.

ARRANGING A THREE-MONTH REVIEW WITH YOUR LINE MANAGER

Check in on your progress and reset any expectations. Plan your next three to six months’ progress and plot the route your Colt career can follow.

KEEP YOUR FOCUS ON BALANCING YOUR STRESS AND ANXIETY LEVELS

You could possibly get frustrated because, realistically, you’re still adjusting to a new job and you don’t know everything yet. But, believe in your abilities, and you’ll be a successful Coltie.

As you start your Colt journey, three months might feel a long way off. But you’ll get through them before you know it and it will seem as if you’ve always been a Coltie. Remember, there are many great tools and resources on the Colt Excel platform that can help you on your journey. You can also interact with other Colties to share your experiences or to read about theirs. We are all here to help you to have the smoothest transition and to become a happy, successful Coltie!
