How do you show us what you’ve got?

Here are three useful steps:

PLAY TO YOUR STRENGTHS

Know your skills, talents and experience. What special knowledge do you have that no one else can offer? Think about how your particular skillset can complement your team’s and boost everyone’s performance.

Check out these Business Skills that could help you:

INFLUENCE, EFFECTIVE MEETINGS and WELLBEING & RESILIENCE.

KNOW THE COMPANY

Make it your mission to know the company and your team from top to bottom. Look for gaps and opportunities to make a difference. Where can you jump in and help? Look at your colleagues’ skills, talents and experience. Who would benefit from some extra support?

Check out these Business Skills that could help you:

COLLABORATION, COMMUNICATION SKILLS and MANAGING RELATIONSHIPS.

TAKE INITIATIVE

You might think you’re overstepping the bounds of your role but be brave. Take a risk! Offer your ideas to your team and see what they think. Turn your ideas into a solid plan of action.

Check out these Business Skills that could help you:

NEGOTATION, ENGAGEMENT and DECISION MAKING.

INNOVATE!

Investigate new possibilities. Find some opportunities for efficiencies or cost savings. Identify an untapped market that’s just waiting for Colt to step in? Think of new products or services that we could offer? Or perhaps a way to reinvent an existing product into something better!

Carl Grivner, our Colt CEO, says that to stay future fit, he reads and researches new technologies and innovations. Stay current with what is happening in your industry.
