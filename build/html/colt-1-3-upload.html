READY, SET, GO!

After being at Colt for a few days, you’ll start to feel more comfortable. Your onboarding journey will continue for a few months. How will you make the most of this experience?

You could just go according to the onboarding structure, or you could take ownership of your experience.

What’s the difference? In both cases you’ll be following process. But, if you take things into your own hands, you’ll also find yourself settling into the Colt culture a lot faster.

Have a look at these 7 tips to help you fast track your onboarding journey:

TIP 1
PAY ATTENTION TO HOW WE TREAT CUSTOMERS

Customers are a priority at Colt, and the faster you learn how to engage with them, the sooner you’ll be able to do this yourself.

Remember: Customers are not just external. Colleagues and Line Managers are internal customers.

TIP 2
GET A FEEL FOR THE TEAM

Wherever you are in Colt, the way you relate to your colleagues will impact how well you work. Make an effort to engage with them, even in small ways (like grabbing them a coffee).

TIP 3
THINK 'CHANGE'

You’re still new, but that shouldn’t stop you from making notes on how you could use your fresh perspective and innovative touch. As soon as you’re ready, you’ll be able to start making a positive impact.

TIP 4
Take note of how every action creates value at Colt

You’ve come across Colt’s success framework and now you need to get familiar with it’s behaviours. As you get up and running, align the things you do with our bigger picture of success.

TIP 5
SET AN EXAMPLE

You can still lead those around you even if you are not in a formal leadership role. During your first weeks this may seem difficult, but it could be as simple as acting in a way that is proactive, honest and enthusiastic.

TIP 6
IDEAS ARE IMPORTANT

If you have an idea that you’d like to share, mention it to peers or a manager. They would be able to help you find if there’s a way it can be put into practice in the Colt context.

TIP 7
GOAL-SETTING STARTS EARLY

As you learn about Colt, start defining your goals with your Line Manager, understanding how you will impact on the success of Colt achieving their goals.

YOU CAN DO IT!

It may be early days for you at Colt but remember that throughout your journey here, it’s always the right time to take initiative and to create and add value.
